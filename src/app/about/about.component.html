About

This website is a personal project of mine that I have been wanting to complete for a very long time. It showcases all the Pokémon cards released from Base Set all the way up to the Sword and Shield era sets. I have utilized the "POKÉMON TCG API" which displays pictures, stats, and various other information from the Pokémon cards released. It is still a work in progress, but hopefully I can at least get one of the sets and most of the pages displayed with the correct information for me and others to view!
